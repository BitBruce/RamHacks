About

What is RamHacks?

RamHacks is a 24-hour programming marathon (hackathon) for any persons of any skill level to design and implement a software and/or hardware application of their choosing. RamHacks offers the opportunity of a collaborative learning environment with mentors and hands-on experience with new technology (link to Tech section here). The Hosted by the Department of Computer Science and Ram Development at VCU, are hosting this hackathon is designed to ignite the spirit of hacking and innovating by providing a competition with no creative boundaries. Check out last year’s inaugural event here!

Who can participate?

Anyone currently enrolled in a college or university and who agrees to the MLH Code of Conduct can participate! Whether you’ve developed a few apps or have never written a line of code, we want you to come!

We're committed to inclusivity and respect for all persons regardless of age, culture, abilities, gender, nationality, race, religion and socioeconomic status. Come one, come all, and bring your friends!

Why participate?

There will be tech talks by some of the sponsors for those interested in learning about new technologies and real world development. There will be a lot of cool hardware to rent for the weekend. Mentors will be available all weekend to help you with any problems. There will be sweet challenges awarded by Major League Hacking and sponsors. There will be Smash Bros. And there will be freakin' awesome food from local Richmond restaurants!
